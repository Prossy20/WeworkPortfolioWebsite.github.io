In the Wework project, we came up with a mobile app called the wework app whereby those that want jobs to be done for them register into the app and login each time they are going to use it. They are then able to post a job while indicating the job type, amount to be paid for the finished job,time they want it done and the location of the job.

On the other hand those that want to take up the posted jobs, have to first register and be verified as agents for security matters. Only those that have qualified as agents are the only ones able to apply for posted jobs and await a notification of acceptance or rejection from the one who posted the job.

why wework project

Due to the increasing cases of unemployment not because they are completely no jobs but rather the difficulty in locating them on the side of those searching for them. On the other hand, they are people who find it difficult to get trustworthy people to get some tasks done and there is no well gazetted platform for this cause.
